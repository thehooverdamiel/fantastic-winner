Al Walters Council # 3230

Al Walters Council 3230 is located at St. John The Baptist Catholic Church in Ypsilanti, Michigan.

The Grand Knight for 2014-2015 is Charley Malewitz.

You can contact the Knights by phone at 734-483-3429, visit our Contact Us page, or Email at knights3230@gmail.com.

Council Meetings

Meetings are held at 7pm on the First Tuesday of every month (or alternate days as needed) in the Knights' room at St.John the Baptist Catholic Church
